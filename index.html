York Hospital Ltd.

যত্নের বুননে সেবা

The journey of York Hospital started with the concept of Boutique Hospital to extend the service one step beyond conventional healthcare. Our personalized care to the patient is more focused on dedicated nursing care and specialized doctors' supervision to give better treatment.

Our Facilities

• One of the best Boutique Hospitals in Bangladesh
• Five storied building with a total of 37,500 SQF area with a lake-side parking facility
• Hospital-wide Wi-Fi network coverage.
• Hospital is under the surveillance of CCTV cameras.
• Muslim prayer area
• Cafeteria
