Consultant

Dr. Iqram A. Rahman

MBBS, D. Ortho (Austria) Ex Associate Professor, Holy Family Red Crescent Medical College and Hospital

Doctor BIO

Dr. Ikram Azizur Rahman completed his graduation from Dhaka Medical College in 1976. Later he achieved his Diploma in Orthopaedics from University of Vienna, Austria in 1983. Dr. Rahman is a member of Bangladesh Medical Council and Life Time Member of Bangladesh Private Practitioner’s Association and American Medical Society of Vienna. Dr. Ikram A Rahman started his career as a Resident Medical Officer in the Department of Orthopaedics in Dhaka Medical College Hospital in 1977 and served in the position until April 1978. From May 1978 to September 1978 he served as a General Duty Doctor at the Ministry of Health of the Government of Iraq; later in December 1978 he was posted in Hamadan, Iran as a General Duty Doctor by the Ministry of Health, Government of Iran, he served in the position until August 1982. From September 1982 to July 1983 he worked as an In-house Trainee at Allgamines Krankenhaus in Vienna, Austria. From October 1983 to January 1993 he served as a Consultant Orthopaedic Surgeon at Ekbatan University Hospital in Hamadan, Iran. From February 1993 to December 2004 he worked as a Consultant in Orthopaedics Department at Holy Family Red Crescent Medical College Hospital. From January 2005 to August 2017 Dr. Rahman served as an Associate Professor in the Department of Orthopaedics at the Holy Family Red Crescent Medical College and Hospital. At present he is working as a Consultant at York Hospital Ltd.
